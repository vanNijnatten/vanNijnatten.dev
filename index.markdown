Bioinformatics is a relative new and exciting field in clinical research. Wet labwork produces gigabytes of data and bioinformaticians apply techniques from data science to find biomarkers that contribute to disease. This website is a personal blog on bioinformatics, tools, algorithms, code, statistics, the struggle to get everything to work and my personal journey to get my PhD. Nothing on this website has been peer-reviewed and my opinion is my own.
